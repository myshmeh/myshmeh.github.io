About

Even though my major is not computer science, I like coding very much! I've interacted with C, Java, Processing, HTML/CSS, little of JavaScript and PHP, and now I am mainly on C# for my self-educational purpose and GML in Game Maker Studio for game development.

My another hobby is music! I like making songs in various genres. L'arc-en-Ciel (Japanese Legendary Rock Band!), Evanescence, Lacuna Coil, Apocalyptica, and Marilyn Manson are my favourites. If you're interested, you can listen to my music on this website!
